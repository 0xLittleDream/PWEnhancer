# 🚀 PW Enhancer v2.0.0 - The Ultimate Update

Welcome to the biggest update for PW Enhancer yet! Version 2.0.0 completely overhauls the extension with brand-new, highly requested features designed to make your Physics Wallah studying experience flawless.

### ✨ What's New in v2.0.0?

🌙 **Smart Dark Mode**
Late-night study sessions just got better. Turn on Dark Mode from the extension popup to instantly transform the entire `pw.live` site into a comfortable, sleek dark theme. The custom CSS filter is smart enough to invert blinding white backgrounds *without* messing up the colors of the videos, thumbnails, or teacher slides!

📸 **Auto-Organized Slide Capture (DRM Bypass)**
Say goodbye to black screens and a messy Downloads folder! Clicking the camera icon on the video player now instantly captures a high-res screenshot of the slide. 
- Automatically bypasses PW's screenshot DRM.
- Automatically creates a master `Physics Wallah` folder in your Downloads.
- Automatically sorts every screenshot into perfectly named sub-folders based on the exact lecture name (e.g., `Downloads/Physics Wallah/Electric Potential/`).

⏱️ **Daily Study Tracker**
A brand new automated tracker in the extension popup! It quietly runs in the background and precisely tracks how much time you spend actively studying on PW (calculating time spent on Notes, DPPs, and Videos). The timer automatically resets to `00h 00m` every single night at midnight.

⚡ **Unlimited Custom Speed**
Break the native speed limit! Use the sleek dropdown menu in the popup to push your video playback speed up to `4.00x`. You can also tweak your speed on the fly using the injected `-` and `+` buttons directly on the video player.

⏳ **TrueTime Pill**
Always know exactly when a lecture will finish. The injected TrueTime Pill actively calculates and displays the *actual* real-world time remaining based on your current custom playback speed. 

🌐 **Universal Compatibility**
The extension now natively supports both **Chrome** and **Firefox** right out of the box.

### 🛠️ Installation
1. Download the `TruetimeExtension.zip` file below.
2. Extract the folder.
3. Go to `chrome://extensions/` (or `about:debugging` in Firefox).
4. Turn on **Developer Mode**.
5. Click **Load Unpacked** and select the extracted folder.

**Enjoy the update and happy studying! 🚀**
